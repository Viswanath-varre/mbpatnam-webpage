About M.B.Patnam

M B Patnam is a small village located in Nathavaram Mandlam situated in Ankapalli District Andhra Pradesh with Zip/Postal code 51115. It is known for its scenic beauty and peaceful environment. The village is surrounded by lush green fields and is home to a close-knit community. The main occupation of the villagers is agriculture, with farming being the primary source of income. M B Patnam is also known for its rich cultural heritage and traditional festivals. The villagers take great pride in their customs and traditions, which are passed down from generation to generation. The village has basic amenities such as schools, roads, drinking water, electricity and healthcare facilities, and a few small businesses. M B Patnam is a place where one can experience the simplicity of rural life and enjoy the beauty of nature.

Key features

Panchayat & Secretariat

M.b.patnam panchayat has two villges under it panchayat (ie.Mallubhupala patnam and A.Sharabavarm) and it's Secretariat is located in m.b.patnam village where its Secretariat(Sachivalayam) code bearing with 10390442. Where it has 12 ward's in panchayat 10 in M.B.Patnam and 2 in A.Sharabavaram. The total geographical area of village is 456 hectares. Mallubhupala Patnam and A.Sharabavaram has a total population of 6,734 peoples, out of which male population is 3,314 while female population is 3,420. There are about 1,786 houses under mallubhupala patnam panchayat. Literacy rate of mallubhupala patnam village is 57.00% out of which 53.72% males and 40.58% females are literate

Agriculture & Animal Husbandry and Dairying

M B Patnam main source of income is based on Agriculture with breathtaking landscapes and scenic beauty with Paddy and Cashew-nuts are major crops where paddy is cultivated in Kharif and Rabi seasons(June to November) and Cashew-nuts is harvested from month of February to May, along with other minor crops like Black-gram(minapa pappu), Green-gram(pesalu) , Red-gram(bobarlu), Sesame seeds(nuvulu) and vegetables. It's other main source of income is through Animal Husbandry and Dairying with sheep's ,goats, Chickens for meet and cows and buffalo's for milking where their are three(3) milk collection centers in village (i.e. Visaka Dairy center 1, Visaka Dairy center 2 & Amul Dairy)
